Very Chocolate Ice Cream

This chocolate ice cream recipe is rich, sweet, creamy, and easy to make with just eight ingredients you likely already have on hand.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Salt
  • Egg yolks
  • Chocolate
  • Heavy cream
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Bring the milk, sugar, cocoa, and salt to a simmer in a saucepan.
  2. Whisk Â½ of the milk mixture into the yolks, return to the pan, and stir until thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chopped chocolate.
  4. Refrigerate in a chilled bowl until cold.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients, then freeze in an ice cream maker.
